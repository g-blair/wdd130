Regular Polytopes

The five 3D Platonic solids are the tetrahedron, the cube, the octahedron, the dodecahedron, and the icosahedron.

The six 4D Platonic solids are the 5-cell, 8-cell, 16-cell, 24-cell, 120-cell, and 600-cell.
